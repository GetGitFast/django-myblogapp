About

Time wasted can never be regained.

A few months back, the company I was working for went into a liquidation. I had no job. There was least possibility of finding a job. For two weeks, I did nothing but hoped for a miracle job. That wasn't going to happen. Early May 2019, I decided to learn new skills. But, I wasn't ready to spend money on any training.

So, I started with free Python lectures on YouTube. It took me around four weeks to come with the grips. Soon, I realised that Python alone wasn't enough. I went on to learn Django,Flask, HTML5, CSS3, and Bootstrap. I don't claim to be an expert but I am pretty satisfied with my progress.

This is my first serious effort to build a website. This website has been built while learning Django. The core of the website is the straight copy of the course framework created by Corey Schafer. But, I have added a number of new features like new pages, new custom styling, fixed footer, populating the Sidebar, introduction of editor for blogs to allow rich text, and much more.

The website is protected against Cross Site Request Forgery through the csrf_token tag. Also, I can guarantee you that I don't get to see your real passwords. All passwords are protected by a cryptographic hash function designed by the United States National Security Agency. A typical password is stored like this: "d9dcx67596gh6ce0xy349c7896x49f09d0b0896b391r3417"

What can you do here? You can express yourself, deliver valuable information and news, make money (if you are lucky), self-promote, connect with like minded people, gain knowledge, and much more...

If your like blogging, then you have reached the right place. Welcome aboard!
