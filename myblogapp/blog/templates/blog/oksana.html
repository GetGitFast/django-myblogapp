Oksana - A book


Blurb

"Twenty one year old Jai Grover has it all: good looks, an engineering degree from IIT-Delhi, rich and loving parents, and an easy life in a sprawling parental home. But when he decides to take a year off to create a dance group and set up an events management company in Mumbai, his life changes forever. He manages to pull together a dance group with the help of five rich teenagers and one Oksana Bzovsky, a beautiful and skilled dancer from Ukraine. Jai faces a number of challenges to win a business for his group. He partly succeeds when opportunities are presented to him by outside help. At the same time, he is dealing with his emerging feelings for Oksana. A sudden change of events leaves him with four options to decide his fate and future with Oksana. Which one will he choose? Fun-filled, romantic and suspenseful, and awash in the vibrancy of modern India, Oksana captures the enchantment of a cross-cultural relationship, the power of friendship, Indian values and the challenges emerging India faces."
